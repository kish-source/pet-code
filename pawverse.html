Business Model

How PawVerse
Makes Money

A multi-stream revenue model built for sustainable, compounding growth across both platforms.

📢
Display & Feed Ads
In-feed native ads and banner placements on PawSocial and VetConnect. Pet brands, food companies, and clinics pay per impression and click. Both pages serve ads independently, doubling inventory.
₹ 8–15L /month potential
⭐
Promoted Posts & Listings
Pet influencers and brands pay to boost posts in PawSocial. Vets and breeders pay for featured placement in VetConnect search results. Tiered pricing from ₹999–₹9999/week.
₹ 5–12L /month potential
💊
Commission on Consults & Meds
15–25% commission on every vet consultation booked. 8–20% margin on medicines and supplements sold. Recurring revenue as pets need regular healthcare and refills.
₹ 20–40L /month at scale
🛍️
Brand Partnerships
Exclusive brand deals where pet companies get a sponsored page, newsletter placement, and category ownership. Annual contracts from ₹2L–₹50L depending on category exclusivity.
₹ 10–25L /month potential
👑
Premium Subscriptions
PawSocial Creator accounts unlock analytics, promo tools, and brand-matching. VetConnect clinics get priority listing, appointment management, and CRM tools. ₹499–₹2999/month.
₹ 3–8L /month potential
🚀
Total Revenue Potential
Combining all streams across both services, at moderate scale (500K MAU), PawVerse targets ₹50–100L/month GMV. The dual-platform model creates multiple compounding revenue loops.
₹ 50–100L /month at 500K users
